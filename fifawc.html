FIFA WORLDCUP 2018 – RUSSIA PREDICTION GAME RULES

Point calculation:

1. Predict match result correctly – 1 point
2. Predict match result with exact scores – 2 points 3. Predict Golden boot winner (Highest goal scorer of the tournament) – 10 points
4. Predict Golden ball winner (Player of the tournament) – 10 points
5. Golden gloves winner ( Best Goalkeeper of the tournament ) - 10 points

Rules and Regulation:

1. For individual match result prediction, you have to predict minimum 12 hour prior match starts. For example if you predict for a match between team X and team Y and that kicks off at 8.30 PM IST, you must predict by 8.30 AM IST same date.
2. For multiple predictions for same match or same topic ( Golden boot, gloves and ball ), your FIRST prediction will be considered
3. For Golden Ball, boot and gloves prediction, you need to predict prior Quarter finals start (Round of 8).
4. Organizing Committee will keep a track of the points scored by individuals and declare Winner, 1st Runner up and 2nd Runner up based on aggregate.
5. Mails should be send only to Pratik Sarangi and Naju Sasikumar.
6. Committee will show the point table in the end of each Rounds. Committee will not show it on any one’s personal interest.
